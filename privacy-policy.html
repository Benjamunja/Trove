Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Trove ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect

Trove is a local-first app. Your memories — photos, videos, audio, and messages — are stored on your device and are never uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly use a future cloud-sync feature.

  • Device storage: All capsule content is stored locally on your device.
  • Location data: Used only to set or trigger location-based unlocks. We do not transmit or store your location.
  • Camera and microphone: Used only when you choose to capture media for a capsule.
  • Purchase data: Handled by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store payment information.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.
  • We do not require an account or login.

Third-Party Services

Trove uses RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect anonymised purchase data as described in their privacy policy.

Data Security

Capsule content is encrypted on your device. You may enable Face ID or Touch ID for additional protection in the app settings.

Children

Trove is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL.
